PHYSICIAN & FOUNDER · FAMILY MEDICINE · DO

Dr. Andrew Burrell, DO

Practicing Since 1990 · Mesa, Arizona

Dr. Burrell founded Greenfield Family Medicine with a simple vision: to provide comprehensive, relationship-based care that treats the whole person, not just the symptoms. With over three decades of experience serving the Mesa community, he has built a practice renowned for its dedication to patient well-being.

His approach combines deep clinical expertise with a genuine interest in his patients' lives, believing that understanding a patient's context is crucial to effective treatment. Specializing in osteopathic medicine, Dr. Burrell integrates structural diagnosis and manipulative therapy into traditional medical care.
