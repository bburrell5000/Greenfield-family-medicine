About Our Practice

A Physician Who Knows Your Name, Not Just Your Chart.

Since opening Greenfield Family Medicine in Mesa, Arizona in 1990, Dr. Andrew Burrell, DO has built a practice rooted in something rare in modern healthcare: genuine, unhurried relationships. As a trusted family doctor in Mesa for over 35 years, Dr. Burrell treats patients of all ages — bringing the same level of care to a child's first checkup as he does to managing complex chronic conditions in seniors.

As a Doctor of Osteopathy, Dr. Burrell takes a whole-person approach to primary care — understanding how lifestyle, environment, and family history all influence your health. Whether you're coming in for an annual physical in Mesa, managing diabetes or hypertension, or exploring newer treatments like StemWave therapy, you'll always find a doctor who listens first.
